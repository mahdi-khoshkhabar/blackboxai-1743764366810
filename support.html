Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my warranty status?

You can check your warranty status by visiting our warranty check page and entering your device's serial number. The serial number can be found in Settings > General > About on your device.

What should I do if my device won't turn on?

First, try charging your device for at least 30 minutes. If it still doesn't turn on, try a force restart. For iPhone 8 or later: Press and quickly release the Volume Up button, press and quickly release the Volume Down button, then press and hold the Side button until you see the Apple logo.

How do I return or exchange a product?

You can initiate a return or exchange within 14 days of receiving your product. Visit our Returns & Exchanges page to start the process. You'll need your order number and the email address used to place the order.

Where can I find Apple Store locations?

You can find all Apple Store locations using our Store Locator. Many locations offer Genius Bar appointments for technical support and repairs.

How do I update my device's software?

For iOS devices: Go to Settings > General > Software Update. For Mac: Go to System Preferences > Software Update. Make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi and has sufficient battery life before updating.
